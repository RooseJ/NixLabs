Features

As a user-friendly focused company, our software is loaded with lots of features to promote an environment where everyone from beginners to professionals can learn. Here are a couple of features to mention a few:

Collaborative Learning

Our simulation software is designed to facilitate collaborative learning, allowing you to work with classmates or colleagues to explore complex systems together.

  • Share your models and simulations with others
  • Learn from each other's successes and mistakes

Assessments and Grading

Our simulation software includes built-in assessments and grading tools that make it easy for educators to evaluate student learning

You can create quizzes and assignments that test students' understanding of simulations, and automatically grade their work based on predefined criteria.

Notifications

Our simulation software includes an email alert feature that notifies you when a simulation has completed, or when a specified event has occurred. Get notified when:

  • A simulation has been assigned.
  • A simulation deadline is close by.
  • Grades have been posted e.t.c.

Gamification

We understand that simulations can be complex and intimidating, so we've added gamification elements to make learning more fun and engaging

Our software includes achievements and leaderboards that encourage you to explore and experiment, and make learning feel like a game.
